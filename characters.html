Characters

Rimuru

A slime that gained unique powers and abilities after reincarnating into a fantasy world.

Benimaru

is a Majin in the service of Rimuru Tempest. He is the Minister of Defense for the Jura Tempest Federation, acting as the Commander-in-Chief for its military during wartime.

Shuna

A is a majin in service to Rimuru Tempest after being named by him. She is Benimaru's younger sister and is Rimuru's chief economic advisor together with Rigurd. She resides in Rimuru City of the Jura Tempest Federation.

Shion

is the First Secretary of Rimuru Tempest and an executive within the Jura Tempest Federation's upper management. She is counted in the ranks of the Twelve Guardian Lords and Big Four, leading her own personal army, the Yomigaeri. She resides in the capital of Tempest, Rimuru City.

Diablo

is one of the seven Primordial Daemons and an Executive of the Jura Tempest Federation, serving as a secretary and Butler to Rimuru Tempest. He is the leader of the Black Numbers and is Rimuru's most powerful subordinate among the Twelve Guardian Lords.

Gobta

is a Hobgoblin originally from the goblin village saved by Rimuru Tempest. He is one of the first martial art students Hakurou took in since the latter became a Kijin. Gobta lives in the capital city of the Jura Tempest Federation: Rimuru City.

Milim

is the daughter of Veldanava and one of the first generation Demon Lords. She's the best friend of Rimuru Tempest and serves as an important ally across the series.

Souei

is a Majin that is in service to Rimuru Tempest after being named by him. He serves as the Minister of Intelligence in Tempest and Rimuru's faithful shadow. He is also the leader of the Shadow Squad of the Jura Tempest Federation.

Hakurou

An elderly swordmaster whose skill was already legendary even as an unnamed ogre, but after being accepted and named by Rimuru he's only gotten stronger.

Ranga

is a magical beast who was named and is now in the service of Rimuru Tempest. He is an extremely loving and loyal subordinate of Rimuru, who almost always resides in his master's shadow and absorbing his energy until called for or when otherwise necessary.
